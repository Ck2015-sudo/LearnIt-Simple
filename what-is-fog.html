What Is Fog? Facts For Kids.

Category: Science

Fog is made of tiny water droplets floating in the air, similar to raindrops.

Fog is a type of low lying cloud that is hard to see through. Because of this, it can be dangerous when driving.

Fog forms when warm air cools down quickly, causing water droplets to appear in the air.

Fun Fact: Did you know some insects and animals use fog to drink water. Some beetles in the desert collect fog on their backs!
