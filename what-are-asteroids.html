What Are Asteroids?

Category: Science

Asteroids are rocky objects that orbit the Sun, mostly found in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter. They can be very small or very large, some even hundreds of kilometers wide.

Asteroids are leftovers from the formation of the solar system. Scientists study them to learn more about how planets were made.

Fun Fact: Some meteorites that land on Earth are pieces of asteroids!
