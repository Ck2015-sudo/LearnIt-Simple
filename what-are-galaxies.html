What Are Galaxies?

Category: Science

Galaxies are massive collections of stars, gas, dust, and dark matter. The Milky Way is the galaxy we live in, and it contains billions of stars, including our Sun.

Galaxies come in different shapes: spiral, elliptical, and irregular. They are moving and can even collide with each other over millions of years.

Fun Fact: Some galaxies are so far away that their light takes millions of years to reach Earth!
