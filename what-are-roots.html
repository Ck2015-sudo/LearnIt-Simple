What Are Roots?

Category: Science

Roots are the part of a plant that grow underground. They take in water and nutrients from the soil and hold the plant in place. Roots also store food for the plant to use later.

Different plants have different types of roots, like deep taproots or spreading roots.

Fun Fact: Some roots, like carrot roots, are actually eaten as food by humans!
