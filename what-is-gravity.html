What Is Gravity?

Category: Science

Gravity is a force that pulls objects toward each other. It keeps us on the ground and makes things fall when we drop them. Gravity also keeps the planets orbiting around the Sun.

The strength of gravity depends on the mass of the objects and how far apart they are. Bigger objects like planets have stronger gravity.

Fun Fact: Gravity is why the Moon orbits the Earth and why tides happen in the ocean!
