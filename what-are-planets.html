What Are Planets?

Category: Science

Planets are large objects that orbit a star. In our solar system, there are eight planets that orbit the Sun. Planets can be rocky, like Earth, or made of gas, like Jupiter.

Planets can have moons, rings, and different climates. Scientists study them to learn about our solar system and the possibility of life elsewhere.

Fun Fact: Some planets spin very fast — Jupiter completes a full spin in just 10 hours!
