Pollinators

Category: Science And Animals

Pollinators are animals that help plants make seeds. Bees, butterflies, birds, and even some bats carry pollen from one flower to another. This helps plants reproduce and grow new plants.

Without pollinators, many of the foods we eat would not exist.

Fun Fact: One bee can visit thousands of flowers in a single day!
