What Are Stars?

Category: Science

Stars are huge balls of burning gas in space. They shine brightly because they give off light and heat. The Sun is the star closest to Earth and is very important for life.

Stars come in different sizes, colors, and brightness. Some are very big, while others are small. They make beautiful patterns in the night sky called constellations.

Fun Fact: Some stars are so big that they could fit millions of suns inside them!
