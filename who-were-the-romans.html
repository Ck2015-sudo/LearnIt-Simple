Who Were The Romans?(Explanation For Kids)

Category: History

The Romans were one of the biggest empires in history. Their empire controlled most of Europe and a small part of Africa.

The capital city of the Roman empire was Rome. The Romans were very smart and could build beautiful structures and are the creators of the famous Aqueduct - a special structure used to take water to large cities.

The Romans had a strong army and easily conquered many lands.

Although the Romans focused on the army and protecting the empire, they still knew how to have fun and relax. Romans enjoyed watching fights at The Colosseum, playing board games, bathing, theatres and animal hunts.
