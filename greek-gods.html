Who Were The Main Greek Gods? Kids History Facts

Category: History

The Greeks had many gods, this text will be covering the Olympian Gods And Goddesses (The Most Known) Who Lived On Mount Olympus - The Tallest Mountain In Greece.

The Greek Gods Were Zeus, Hera, Poseidon, Athena, Demeter, Apollo, Hephaestus, Hestia, Ares, Artemis, Aphrodite And Hermes.

Zeus was the king of the gods, his wife Hera was the goddess of marrige, Athena was the goddess of wisdom, Demeter was the goddess of harvest, Apollo was the god of light, Hephaestus was the god of fire, Hestia was the goddess of hearth (Home, Family), Ares was the god of war, Artemis was the goddess of hunting, Aphrodite was the goddess of love and Hermes was the messenger god
