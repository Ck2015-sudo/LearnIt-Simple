10 Amazing Space Facts for Kids

Category: Science And Fun Facts

  1. The Sun is a star. It’s a giant ball of hot gas called hydrogen.
  2. There are eight planets in our solar system and six big dwarf planets.
  3. Some planets, like Jupiter, are mostly gas.
  4. Stars are very far away — light takes years to reach us from some stars.
  5. Black holes have super strong gravity. Even the fastest thing in the universe, light, can't escape.
  6. There are over 300 moons in the Solar System, but most are small rocks about the size of a town.
  7. There are over 100 Billion galaxies in the universe. That's more galaxies than all the people on Earth combined!
  8. According to scientists' estimations, there are 50 Sextillion (50,000,000,000,000,000,000,000) planets in the universe like Earth - That's more than every grain of sand on Earth combined!
