What Is Photosynthesis?

Category: Science

Photosynthesis is how plants make their own food. They take sunlight, water, and air, and turn it into sugar and oxygen. This process gives energy for the plant to grow.

Without photosynthesis, there would be no plants, and life on Earth would be very different!

Fun Fact: Plants produce about half of the oxygen we breathe through photosynthesis!
