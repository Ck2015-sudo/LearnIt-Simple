How Trees Grow

Category: Science

Trees start as seeds that grow into seedlings. Over many years, they develop roots, a trunk, branches, and leaves. Trees can live for hundreds or even thousands of years.

Trees provide oxygen, shade, homes for animals, and wood for humans to use. They are very important for life on Earth.

Fun Fact: The tallest tree in the world is over 115 meters high!
