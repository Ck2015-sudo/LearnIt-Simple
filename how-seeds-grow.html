How Seeds Grow

Category: Science

Seeds are baby plants. When they get water, warmth, and soil, they start to grow. First, roots come out to find water, then a tiny shoot grows toward the sunlight.

With time and care, the seed grows into a full plant that can produce flowers, fruits, or more seeds.

Fun Fact: Some seeds can stay asleep in the soil for years before they start growing!
