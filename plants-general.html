Plants: A Simple Guide

Category: Science

Plants are living things that grow from the ground. They make their own food using sunlight, water, and air. Plants come in many shapes and sizes, from tiny moss to huge trees.

Plants provide oxygen, food, and shelter for animals and people. They are very important for life on Earth.

Fun Fact: Some plants can live for hundreds or even thousands of years!
