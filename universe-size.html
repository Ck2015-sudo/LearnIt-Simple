How Big Is the Universe?

Category: Science

The universe is incredibly huge — much bigger than we can imagine. It contains billions of galaxies, each with billions of stars and planets. Light from the farthest galaxies takes billions of years to reach us.

Scientists study the universe using telescopes and space probes to learn more about stars, planets, and galaxies. There is still a lot we don’t know!

Fun Fact: If the universe were a race track, light traveling from one end to the other would take longer than the age of the Earth!
