Amazing Facts About Cacti

Category: Science

Cacti are special plants that live in deserts. They store water in their thick stems and have spines instead of leaves to keep water inside. Cacti can survive very hot and dry conditions.

Some cacti bloom with beautiful flowers that last only a short time.

Fun Fact: The tallest cactus in the world is over 20 meters tall!
