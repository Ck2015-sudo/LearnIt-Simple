Why Are Leaves Green?

Category: Science

Leaves are green because of a chemical called chlorophyll. Chlorophyll helps plants catch sunlight to make food through photosynthesis. Without it, plants could not survive.

Leaves can change color in the fall when chlorophyll breaks down, showing reds, oranges, and yellows.

Fun Fact: Chlorophyll is why plants not only eat sunlight but also give us oxygen!
